With his cherubic aloofness and low-key style, Ryan Noack makes Winnie the Pooh look like an uptight banker. Drawing upon his experiences as a Special Education teacher, Ryan’s biting wit and natural likeability have made him a popular feature around the Northwest. He regularly performs at Laughs Unlimited, the Comedy Underground, and Liquid Laughs, and has opened for Tim Northern, Bengt Washburn, and Todd Glass. Ryan currently resides in Boise, Idaho, where he skis, coaches baseball, and occasionally works for honey. You don’t need Ryan Noack to laugh, but it helps.
